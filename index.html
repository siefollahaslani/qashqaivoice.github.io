Welcome to QashqAI Voice

This is a digital initiative dedicated to preserving endangered dialects and promoting inclusion through open technology.

What is QashqAI Voice?

QashqAI Voice is a community-led project focused on giving forgotten languages a place to speak again — using AI tools like Whisper, GPT, and TTS to make language accessible, audible, and alive.
